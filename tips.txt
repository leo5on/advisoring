create a new workspace "ros-warthog"
source the new workspace into the .bashrc
clone the warthog stuff from github into the src directory
	https://github.com/warthog-cpr/warthog.git
	https://github.com/warthog-cpr/warthog_simulator.git



to open the warthog with gazebo:
	roslaunch warthog_gazebo warthog_world.launch

control warthog through the keyboard:
	rosrun teleop_twist_keyboard teleop_twist_keyboard.py

control warthog with the joystick:
	rosrun joy joy_node
	rosrun advisoring warthog_teleop.py

launch everything with a simple command:
	roslaunch advisoring warthog.launch
